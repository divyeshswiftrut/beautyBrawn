Dr. Catherine Uram is an experienced physician with over 13 years in medicine. She began her career in the Emergency Room and later established a private integrated medicine practice, providing holistic patient care. Her versatile experience includes telemedicine, supervision, and collaboration with nurses.

Driven by her passion for medicine and patient well-being, Dr. Uram expanded into aesthetics and advanced medical treatments. She's trained in cosmetic neurotoxins, dermal fillers, IV nutritional therapies, and more. Dr. Uram is dedicated to patient safety and has completed a Medical Director course for lasers and aesthetics.

With a creative touch and a commitment to helping patients look and feel their best, she's a valuable addition to any med spa team. Her holistic approach and extensive experience make her an ideal medical director collaborator.

How It Works?

Botox, Dysport, Xeomin and other neurotoxins target the underlying causes of wrinkles and frown lines. We inject small doses into the end of a nerve to temporarily block its impulses and allow facial muscles to relax.

Treatment only takes 10-15 minutes, and a few tiny injections. You can be in and out on your lunch break! Noticeable results should appear 3-5 days after treatment, with full results visible 14 days post treatment.

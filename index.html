About Us

Meet Jerissa, the heartbeat of our medspa. With over 8 years of nursing experience in the ICU and Emergency Room, as well as 5 years as a pilates and lagree fitness instructor, Jerissa has always had a passion for helping people feel their best. With that passion for personalized care, she quickly realized aesthetics was an incredible way to help people feel their best, inside and out. Jerissa is dedicated to enhancing your natural beauty, and making sure you have a comfortable, and transformative experience. Trust her with your aesthetic journey, and discover the perfect blend of skill, compassion, and expertise.

Our Statement

At Beauty and Brawn Aesthetics, we are dedicated to helping you look and feel your best, both inside and out. Our approach is all about you, and we’re committed to delivering exceptional results that empower you to embrace your beauty and strength. Every journey begins with a one-on-one consultation.
